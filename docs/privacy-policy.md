# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** July 20, 2026

HabitLoop ("we", "our", or "us") is a free, open-source habit tracker app. This privacy policy explains what data the app accesses, how it's used, and your rights.

## Data Storage

All habit data (habits, completions, streaks) is stored **locally on your device only**. No habit data is ever sent to our servers or any third party. Your data never leaves your device except through the explicit export feature you initiate.

## Data Collected by Third Parties

### Google AdMob
The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
- Advertising ID
- Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
- IP-derived approximate location (country/region level)
- App usage data for ad targeting

This data is collected by Google, not by us. We do not have access to this data.

### Google Play Billing
If you purchase the "Remove Ads" option, Google Play Billing processes the transaction. We do not collect or store any payment information.

## Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS**: For optional habit reminders you choose to set
- **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED**: To reschedule reminders after device restart

We do not request or use any other permissions.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with anyone. The only data transmitted from the app is anonymized ad analytics through Google AdMob, which you can opt out of via the app's Privacy Options.

## Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
